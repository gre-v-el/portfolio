All is Ball

January-February 2024

See website
Flutter
Flame
Google Play

A proof-of-concept game based on The Suika Game. My original spin on the idea is making it radial and making the player drop many balls at once. There are 7 levels, each with more balls to unlock. The game ends when the ball clump becomes too big to fit in the screen. The game is available on Google Play.
